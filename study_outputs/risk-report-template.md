### Wording rules the system must enforce
- Always: *"shows patterns often associated with…"*  ·  Never: *"your child has…"*
- Never name a specific disorder (dyslexia / dysgraphia / ADHD) as a conclusion.
- Always end with the disclaimer above.
- Prefer catching at-risk children (high sensitivity); a false alarm only costs one professional conversation.
